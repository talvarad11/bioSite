Kelly's Hobbies

Kelly has enjoyed playing golf most of her life and was able to share some time on the links with her daughter on Mothers Day one year.
The weather was not perfect but they both had an amazing time. Kelly cherishes these times she gets to spend with her children making memories and sharing hobbies.

The weather gets pretty hot here in Nebraska over the Summer so Kelly gets to the pool anytime she can.
The kids are both water babies, although, Isabel was particularly tired today so she fell asleep while floating around on the water.

During a golf outing, Kelly won a raffle prize that provided a trip to Estes Park, Colorado. Kelly and Tim took advantage of this time away and took the trip
in February of 2020. There was hiking, brewery visits, and a trip to the Stanley Hotel for a haunted tour and whiskey tasting.
